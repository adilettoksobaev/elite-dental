Cosmetic Dentistry
Cosmetic dentistry is generally used to refer to any dental work that improves the appearance of teeth, gums and/or the bite of the teeth and jaw. It primarily focuses on improving dental aesthetics in color, position, shape, size, alignment, and overall smile appearance.
Veeners

Veneers are a form of cosmetic dentistry created mostly for aesthetic reasons. Veneers are a layer of material placed over a tooth.

They improve the aesthetics of a smile and/or protect the toothâ€™s surface from damage. Veneers are made in a dental laboratory and are created from a thin layer of porcelain that is cemented on the surface of the tooth.

Veneers are recommended in cases of:
Abrasions
Erosions
Discoloration
Anomalies
Simple aesthetic reasons
