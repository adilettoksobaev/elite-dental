Dr. Andre Eliasian

Dr. Andre Eliasian is consistently among the best dentists in Glendale, CA. He is an award-winning dental professional, an active community volunteer, and the owner of Elite Dental Group Corporation.

He is passionate about providing cosmetic dentistry to the greater Los Angeles area and at his core, Dr.Eliasian strives to transform the lives of his patients. He is fluent in Armenian, Russian, and English, and has earned the trust of people from different communities over the years.

His growing roster of patients spans the areas of Burbank, Glendale, Tujunga, La Crescenta, La Canada, Montrose, and Pasadena.

Get to know us

Our office is located in Glendale, CA and whether you need affordable dental implants, wisdom tooth removal, or cosmetic dentistry, we are here for you!

Our goal is to cultivate a place of relaxation and to serve you with the best dental care. We pride ourselves in creating a space of ease that emulates a dental spa experience. Your experience is our priority and we offer custom dental packages to fit your specific needs.
