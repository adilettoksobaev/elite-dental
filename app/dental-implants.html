Dental Implants
Losing a tooth can drastically affect your quality of life because it can cause discomfort as well as change the aesthetic balance of your smile.
One of our main goals is to preserve your natural teeth, however, sometimes depending on the severity of tooth decay, intervention is necessary.
Dental implant supported fixed prosthesis

Implant-supported fixed restorations are a treatment option for patients who have lost all of their teeth. These restorations are attached to surgically placed implants and are not removable. They are made of high-strength materials like zirconia.

Many years ago, when people did not have any tooth structure left, the only options were dentures. Nowadays, after only placing 6 to 8 implants, people have the chance to create a full fixed smile.

After the implants are surgically placed and healed, the dental laboratory fabricates a full-arch dental bridge that is made of strong, natural-looking material. The bridge is fixed to the implants, creating a full smile.

Dental implants are one of the best ways to custom build new teeth to restore the alignment of the original smile with the same aesthetic and functional capabilities of natural teeth.

Dental implants are small metal anchors made of titanium that have the unique ability to fuse to the jawbone and provide a strong foundation.

At Elite Dental Group, we use top tier dental implant brands such as Straumann, Nobel Biocare; to name a couple.

We always prioritize patient comfort and safety while still maintaining the integrity of your smile.

Immediate Dental Implants

Immediate implants make it possible to do several procedures during the same visit and have proven to be one of the top innovations in dentistry.

  • After extracting a tooth, the jawbone underneath starts to shrink back. Placing an implant at the same time creates the benefit of keeping the maximum amount of jawbone and having more support for implants; which make it possible to restore the smile in just a few hours.
  • If the jaw does not have enough bone to place a long-lasting implant, there might be a need for a bone graft procedure.

We offer the placement of the bone graft within the same visit and this creates a stronger foundation for the long-term success of the implants. You can only do this treatment if there is a need for a bone graft due to bone loss.

Dental Implant Supported Removable Dentures

Elite Dental Group offers Dental Implant Supported Removable Dentures that are fixed in the mouth and do not move. DISRDs are a great affordable way to restore a patient’s smile.

The first procedure for having a fixed denture is the placement of the dental implant. Depending on the case, we may need to place 2 to 4 implants in the jawbone.

On the same day, we provide our patients with temporary dentures. After a few follow-up visits, the mold is taken and the fixable denture is fabricated.
