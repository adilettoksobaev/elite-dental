Dr. Andre Eliasian
Dr. Eliasan is an acclaimed Armenian dentist in Glendale, CA who is fluent in Armenian and has served the dental needs of the Armenian community and other Glendale residents for many years.

Dr. Eliasian has a Doctorate in Dental Surgery and has a reputation for helping his patients achieve “the perfect smile” by restoring missing and damaged teeth using the latest in Dental Implant technology – the best choice for a natural smile.

He is an experienced dental implant specialist, tooth whitening dentist, and oral surgeon who is passionate about providing cosmetic dentistry to Burbank, Glendale, Tujunga, La Crescenta, La Canada, Montrose and Pasadena residents.

He is also part of the network of dentists that accepts PPO insurance.

We Create Dream Smiles

His professional career began with an Undergraduate Degree in Biology at California State University, Northridge (CSUN) in 2007. He earned his advanced degree – Doctorate in Dental Surgery – from the prestigious New York University (NYU) in 2013.

Dr. Andre Eliasian is an active member of multiple dental affiliations, including the California Dental Association (CDA) and the American Dental Association (ADA).

As a top dental implant specialist, Dr. Eliasian is a member of the International Congress of Oral Implantology, the American Academy of Implant Dentistry, the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry and the American Academy of General Dentistry.
