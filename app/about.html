About us
Elite Dental Group by Dr. Andre Eliasian Serving the community by creating dream smiles through an exceptional dental practice.
Dr. Andre Eliasian

Dr.Eliasian’s professional career began with an Undergraduate Degree in Biology at California State University, Northridge (CSUN) in 2007.

He then went on to earn his advanced Doctorate in Dental Surgery from the prestigious New York University (NYU) in 2013. Through his training and dedication, he is an active member of multiple dental affiliations, including the California Dental Association (CDA) and the American Dental Association (ADA).

As a top dental implant specialist, Dr. Eliasian is a member of the International Congress of Oral Implantology, the American Academy of Implant Dentistry, the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, and the American Academy of General Dentistry.

Dr.Elisasian works diligently to stay educated on the most innovative dental care through advanced training courses in cosmetic, implant, and restorative dentistry. As a leader in his field, he is always refining his techniques for dental implants, wisdom tooth extraction, and other dental procedures.

Although he has a Doctorate in Dental Surgery, his reputation for helping his patients achieve their “dream smile" goes beyond restoring missing and damaged teeth; his integrity also shines through his work.
